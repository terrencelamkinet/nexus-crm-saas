Core modules shown with generated examples

This page explains the product modules using synthetic scenarios only, so you can present the UX safely before connecting real CRM records.

Calendar classifierEvery 15 min

Assigns meeting intent labels and maps events to the most likely account.

  • Flags external client meetings
  • Skips internal and personal events
  • Prepares match candidates
Nudge engineTwo reminders

Sends a preparation nudge at 60 minutes and a final alert at 15 minutes.

  • Delivery by chat or desktop
  • Prioritised by relationship heat
  • Quiet for low-value noise
Meeting briefPre-meeting

Builds a generated briefing pack with open tasks, last touchpoints, and suggested talk tracks.

  • Owner reminder summary
  • Opportunity stage recap
  • Suggested next-step prompts
Post-meeting loggerAfter meeting

Prompts the user to confirm notes and create a touchpoint if nothing has been logged.

Deal radarMorning scan

Highlights stalled deals, overdue actions, and accounts with falling health scores.

Relationship coachWeekly review

Suggests which accounts should receive a call, email, or meeting invite next.
