Architecture page

A clean page for the product loop

This page documents the route loop and implementation flow so the package does not break when deployed as a static multi-page website.

1

Collect

Events, tasks, companies, and contacts enter through native systems or adapters.

2

Normalize

Records are cleaned into a consistent model before scoring and matching.

3

Reason

The AI layer ranks urgency, asks for clarification, and drafts follow-up context.

4

Act

The system delivers nudges, meeting packs, and relationship actions.

5

Write back

Approved actions sync back into the CRM and calendar state.

6

Review

Users open the next page, module, or source file without broken links.
